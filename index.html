String Guide (Courses 6 → 1)

Tip: Tune from low C (Course 6) upward. Touch “Play” to hear a reference tone. When the bubble is centered and green, you’re in tune (±3¢).

Why does the tuner jump or fail to lock?

Rooms with lots of noise, very soft plucks, or plucking both strings in a course can confuse any tuner. Try a firmer pluck and mute nearby strings. Hold the phone 20–30 cm (8–12 inches) from the rosette.
